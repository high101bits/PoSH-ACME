When surveying Windows systems, obtain as much info as possible to establish a baseline:
......ID Systems used by Administrators and Power Users
......ID User Accounts and Group Memberships
......Number of Hosts and Servers
......Versions of Operating Systems
......Installed Service Packs and Security Updates/Patches
......Installed Software
......Expected Software / Programs Used
......Startup Software / Autoruns
......Processes
......Services
......Driver Info
......Number of Network Inteface Cards (dual or more NICs)
......Source and Destination IPs
......Ports and Protocols
......DNS Cache
......Firewall Status and Rules
......Mapped Drives and Shares
......Group Policy
......Domain Trusts
